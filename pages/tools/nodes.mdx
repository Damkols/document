Mint Blockchain provides a JSON-RPC interface that allows developers and users to interact with the blockchain and perform various actions, such as sending transactions, querying account balances, and more.

<b>Mint Mainnet JSON-RPC URL</b>: 

https://rpc.mintchain.io

https://global.rpc.mintchain.io

https://asia.rpc.mintchain.io


<b>Mint Sepolia Testnet JSON-RPC URL</b>: 

https://sepolia-testnet-rpc.mintchain.io


If your project needs better RPC performance, you can try to deploy your own Mint Blockchain node. Check all the information here: [Node Operators](/tools/operators)
